What is VOCALOID?

VOCALOID is a singing voice synthesizer software product designed by Yamaha Corporation and Music technology Group. The software was ultimately developed into the commercial product that was released in 2004.

What is a Vocal Synthesizer?

A Vocal Synthesizer is a computer program that is used to create a human voice. The main types of vocal synthesizers are text-to-speech, voice conversion, and singing synthesizers. The type of synths I will be focusing on are VOCALOID singing synthesizers.
